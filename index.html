I am a PhD student in the FLOWERS team at Inria Bordeaux, supervised by Pierre-Yves Oudeyer and Clément Moulin-Frier. I use curiosity-driven AI to discover novel phenomena in artificial-life systems — cellular automata in particular. My broader interests sit at the intersection of machine learning, evolutionary algorithms, reinforcement learning, and cognitive science, and I am fascinated by emergence in all its forms.
